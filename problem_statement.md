# Revolut analytical challenge

## Context

You are a member of the team responsible for profitability at Revolut. The team tracks total gross profit in the last 90 days (GP L90D) as its primary metric. Given Revolut's rapid changes to product offering, the team is exploring whether recent initiatives are negatively impacting GP L90D.

Gross Profit = Revenue - Cost of Sales

## Objective

The team is looking to increase GP L90D by £4 million in the next 6-12 months. You have been tasked with providing recommendations to achieve this target based on your findings.

## Dataset

User-level revenues and costs in the last 90 days. Key columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| user_id | Unique user identifier |
| pnl_date | Date for which revenues and costs are recorded |
| user_city, user_country | Country and city of residence |
| account_level_1 to account_level_4 | P&L account hierarchy for structuring management P&L statements |
| amount_gbp | Gross profit generated by a user, measured in £1,000s |
| user_type | Personal, Youth, Business, Business_User, Business_Employee, Company |
| age_group | Pre-defined age groups |
| onboarded_date | Date when user completed onboarding |
| user_state | Active or Inactive |
| user_plan | Current plan (Standard, Plus, Premium, Metal, Ultra for personal; Basic, Grow, Scale, Custom for business) |
| is_nap | NAP = New Active Person, has spent at least £20 and is eligible for vending machine reward |
| pnl_month | Month of the P&L entry |

## Recent initiatives

### Revolut Retail
1. Credit team reduced interest rates on loans (Romania, Germany, Lithuania, all plans)
2. Savings team increased interest rates on instant access savings for personal customers (all countries, Metal and Ultra plans)
3. Lifestyle team launched eSIMs with a third-party vendor (available to everyone)
4. New pricing for Stays, reducing cashback by 30% on each transaction
5. Bank Payments in HUF and RON: first 2 transfers per month free for Premium (previously all paid)
6. Growth team ran vending machine card campaign: users pick up cards, spend £20 to become NAP, receive monetary reward
7. Subscription team rolled out new benefits in Plus and Ultra plans (partner subscriptions: video streaming, health and wellness apps)
8. Card payment vendors introduced changes in cost structure for personal customer payments (ATM withdrawals, top-ups)
9. New vendor for creating and shipping cards (100% of volume, all countries, all card types)

### Revolut Business
10. Simplified business onboarding (fewer steps)
11. New terminal for offline payments (payment processing, restaurant orders, point-of-sale)
12. Money Market Funds for businesses (highest interest rate on market, all plans except Basic)
13. New Scale plan launched in March for larger businesses (discounted FX)

### Other
14. Improved refund settlement speed for UK and Ireland (3 hours)
15. Legal team streamlined initiative launch process (50% decrease in approval time)
16. New regulation: German loan provisions increased from 5% to 10% of face value
17. Built in-house SMS notification system (offboarding vendor)

## Deliverables

- Summary output in PDF format (max 10 pages), presentable to executive leadership team
- Show all work including code
- Specify any assumptions made
